Manage Categories For Your Odoo POS Products
Managing plenty of products in your store in POS is definitely a difficult task. And, placing them correctly into different categories is undoubtedly important to address.
Choosing the right category is of utmost importance so that customers can easily find your product in no time which leads you to increase in sales and profits.
Moreover, it is possible that a product may fall under more than one category; hence, the module gives you a feature to manage more than one category for your Odoo POS Products.
Assign multiple categories to the product in Odoo
While creating the product add the name of the product. Then navigate to the ‘Point Of Sale’ tab and go to the Point Of Sale Categories. You can assign multiple categories to this product.
The users can assign multiple categories to single products. After that, the corresponding product will appear in various categories in the POS.
